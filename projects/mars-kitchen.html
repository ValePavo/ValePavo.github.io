About the Project

Mars Kitchen is a virtual reality cooking simulation game that takes place in a retro-futuristic Martian setting. Players experience the challenge of preparing meals in a VR kitchen environment on Mars, combining realistic cooking mechanics with an immersive sci-fi atmosphere. Released on Meta Quest and PlayStation VR platforms.

My Technical Contributions

Complex Physics Management

Leveraged tailored rigid body algorithms and efficient physics calculations to ensure smooth, immersive gameplay on standalone devices like Meta Quest and PlayStation VR

AI Virtualization

Engineered an AI Virtualization System to simulate off-screen customer behavior and order generation. This maintained complex game state consistency with minimal CPU overhead

Shader & VFX

Created custom shaders and particle systems to ensure a distinctive and high-performance visual feedback

Runtime Lightmap Swapping

Architected a custom system to enable a seamless “Endless Mode”. This allowed for instant environment changes without scene reloading, maintaining player immersion while managing memory constraints

Performance Optimization

Created a custom impostor system to load distant meshes and save signifant frames and performed profiling and debugging to maintain high frame rates and ensure the stability of the physics engine

PSVR Porting

Managed the technical porting to PS5 (PSVR2), integrating hardware-specific features and PSN features (Activities, Thophies, ecc.) to successfully navigated strict Sony TRC standards to ensure a smooth launch on the PlayStation Store
